本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪服务。我们不会将您的数据分享给任何第三方。

所有本地存储的数据受 iOS 系统级数据保护机制保护。我们建议您启用设备密码保护以增强安全性。

Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Last updated: June 24, 2026

PokerMind GTO ("we", "us", or "the App") is a decision training tool. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data in this App (including hand records, training scores, preferences, etc.) is stored locally on your device using Apple SwiftData / UserDefaults frameworks. We do not upload this data to any server.

2. Payment Information

All payment transactions are processed directly by Apple via the StoreKit 2 framework. We do not access, store, or transmit your payment information (such as credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.). Payment credentials are verified device-side via Apple's JWS signatures.

3. Information Collected by Apple

This App is distributed via Apple's App Store. Apple may collect anonymous crash logs, usage statistics, etc. to improve App Store services. This data collection is governed by Apple's privacy policy. For details, see Apple Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third party.

5. Data Security

All locally stored data is protected by iOS system-level data protection mechanisms. We recommend enabling device passcode protection for enhanced security.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13. This App does not include any features designed to intentionally collect personal information from children.
